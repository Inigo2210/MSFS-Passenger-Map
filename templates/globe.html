⚠️ Switching Views

Switching to the 2D map will reset everything — your current route, flight trail, and all progress will be lost. You will need to reload your route in the new view.

If you are switching to the 3D globe, please note that it uses more system resources than the 2D map. You may see lagging or slower responses. Additionally, using the globe map may take system resources away from your PC therefore impacting MSFS performance, and on mobile devices the battery may discharge faster than normal.
